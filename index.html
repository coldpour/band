Bio

Red Karma 13 is a five-piece band pushing the edges of modern rock with pulsating grooves, hazy cinematic textures, and anthemic hooks. Their live shows feel like a deep-space ritual: immersive visuals, hypnotic rhythms, and a wall of sound that begs you to move.

Drawing inspiration from desert psych, city neon, and late-night radio, the band builds songs that travel from introspective calm to cathartic release. Every set is designed to make you feel part of the story.
